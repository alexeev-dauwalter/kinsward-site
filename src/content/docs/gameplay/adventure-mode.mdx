Adventure mode puts you in direct control of a single character. Move through the world, mine blocks, place structures, and interact with items in real time — all presented in a dark-themed UI with floating transparent panels.

## HUD

The top-left corner shows a floating HUD overlay (no background):

- **Level** badge — large number showing the unit's current level
- **HP** bar — red health bar with current/max values
- **EP** bar — green energy bar with current/max values

## Movement

Move with <Kbd linux="W" /> <Kbd linux="A" /> <Kbd linux="S" /> <Kbd linux="D" /> (North/West/South/East). Hold <Kbd linux="Shift" /> to sprint.

### Z-Level Navigation

The world has vertical z-levels. Navigate between them using stairs:

- <Kbd linux="Shift+," /> — climb stairs up
- <Kbd linux="Shift+." /> — descend stairs down

Stairs Up and Stairs Down are constructable blocks that can be placed in Overseer mode's Build Menu under the Stairs category.

Your character stands at z=N on top of a solid block at z=N-1. The block at your feet (z=N) is usually AIR.

### Surface Transitions

When moving between tiles at different elevations:

| Transition | Condition |
|-----------|-----------|
| Walk | Same z-level |
| Step Up | Target is 1 block higher |
| Step Down | Target is 1 block lower |
| Fall | Target is >1 block lower (damage planned) |
| Blocked | Target has `blocks_movement` at feet level |

A tile is walkable if it does **not** have `blocks_movement` at feet level **and** has `supports_standing` below.

## Block Interaction

- **Left click** — mine the block at your cursor
- **Right click** — place the selected hotbar item as a block

### Z-Level Targeting

By default, clicks target the wall level (z, same as your character). Use modifiers to target other levels:

| Modifier | Level | Cursor Color |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| None | Wall (z) | White |
| <Kbd linux="Ctrl" /> | Floor (z-1) | Blue |
| <Kbd linux="Alt" /> | Ceiling (z+1) | Orange |

Additional cursor colors: **red** = block can be mined, **green** = block can be placed.

## Inventory & Hotbar

Your character carries items in a **4x10 grid** (40 slots) with a visible hotbar of 10 slots.

- <Kbd linux="1" /> through <Kbd linux="0" /> — select hotbar slots
- **Scroll wheel** — cycle through hotbar
- <Kbd linux="Tab" /> — open/close full inventory
- <Kbd linux="Q" /> — drop item
- <Kbd linux="Shift" /> + click — move full stack

The inventory panel also displays:

- **Equipment slots** — Head, Weapon, Body, Shield, Legs, Accessory (6 slots on the left)
- **Stats** — STR, DEX, CON, INT, WIS, CHA displayed below equipment
- **Traits** — trait pills shown at the bottom of the inventory

:::note
Equipment slots and stat modifiers are visible in the UI but not yet functional. They will become active with the attribute system in a future release.
:::

Items can be block types (Stone Wall, Wood Wall, Glass Wall, etc.) which can be placed with right click, or resources (Wood, Apple) for crafting.

## Crafting

Press <Kbd linux="C" /> to open the crafting panel. It shows the active crafting station (or "Hand" if none nearby) and available recipes. Each recipe displays ingredient counts as have/need (e.g. "2/4 Stick"). Click a recipe to craft it. See [Crafting](/gameplay/crafting/) for details.

## Companions

Press <Kbd linux="N" /> to open the Companions panel. See [Units & Companions](/gameplay/units/) for companion behaviors and management.

## Pause Menu

Press <Kbd linux="Escape" /> to open the pause menu:

- **Continue** — resume the game
- **Save World** — save to a timestamped slot
- **Load** — browse and load from a list of all saves with formatted dates
- **Quit** — exit to desktop

Autosave runs every 5 minutes, rotating through 5 slots. The Load menu lists all saves including autosaves.

## Switching Modes

Press <Kbd linux="B" /> to switch to [Overseer Mode](/gameplay/overseer-mode/). Hold <Kbd linux="B" /> for 0.3 seconds or more to quick-peek into Overseer mode — release to snap back to Adventure mode. While peeking, you can interact with Overseer panels.
